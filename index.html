Recycle PE Foam Program

For your convenience, a mail-back procedure for recycling your Cushion Foam Sheets has been established.

Recycle Now!

How to Recycle

To facilitate shipping from home, the United States Postal Service (USPS) provides numerous options for printing labels and/or postage using online resources. This convenience also allows you to have the shipment picked up by your local USPS carrier as outbound mail.

Step One: Ensure the PE foam is free of tape, labels or any foreign materials.
Step Two: Identify the nearest Recycle PE Foam return location to you by selecting a Zone from the map below. Here you will find the listing of all participating fabricators who accept PE foam.
Step Three: Prepare the shipping label and affix postage for delivery to the nearest return location. Please note all shipments must be prepaid, COD’s will not be accepted.

Ship “RecyclePEFoam” to any location in the recycling network. Please mark Attn: Recycle PE Foam on the shipping box. All shipments must be prepaid, COD's will not be accepted. Polyethylene materials must be free of tape, labels or any foreign materials.
